My Work

During my collegiate career I've had the opportunity to do several internships at some great companies.

The first company I worked for as an intern was Catalyst Repository Systems during the summer of 2013. While I was there I worked as a Product Support Developer, and got to learn Python and WSGI along the way. This was an awesome enviornment to work in, and I had a great boss. I also enjoyed working in Denver, Colorado; which is where they are located.

In the summer of 2014 I stayed in Lincoln, Nebraska to do an internship at the small start-up company ComplyTrax. Here I worked along with the head developer to work on the middleware and front end of their system. The primary technology stack at ComplyTrax was ASP.NET web forms, and a major project during my time there was refractoring to ASP.NET MVC.

During my time as a student at Union College I've also worked to help start a technology club on campus. My role in this club was to help plan programming events such as hackathons. We also partnered with a local school to volunteer teaching programming to students K-12.
